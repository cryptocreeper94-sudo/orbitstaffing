## 1. Agreement & Binding Nature

By downloading the ORBIT mobile app and clicking "I Agree," you enter into a binding agreement with ORBIT Staffing OS and the employer using ORBIT.

**This agreement is legally binding.** It governs your use of the ORBIT app, how your data is used, and your rights and responsibilities as a worker.

---

## 2. Services ORBIT Provides

ORBIT provides a mobile platform that allows you to:
- Browse available shifts posted by employers
- Accept shifts and receive assignments
- Clock in and out of shifts using GPS verification
- Track your hours and earnings
- Receive payments via direct deposit
- Contact your employer for support
- Access your work history and timesheets

**ORBIT is a technology platform, not an employer.** Your employer is the entity that posts jobs and assigns work. ORBIT facilitates the connection.

## 3. Your Responsibilities as a Worker

### 3.1 Honest & Accurate Information
- You must provide accurate personal information (name, address, SSN, contact info)
- You must keep your information current
- You are responsible for any errors caused by providing false information
- You agree not to create multiple accounts or impersonate others

### 3.2 Device & Account Security
- You are responsible for your phone and ORBIT login credentials
- Keep your password secure and don't share it
- If your phone is lost or stolen, contact your employer immediately
- You are liable for any actions taken using your account/phone
- ORBIT is not liable for unauthorized use if security was compromised by your negligence

### 3.3 Truthful Location Data
- You must clock in and out at the actual job location
- Do not use fake GPS, spoofing apps, or VPNs to fake your location
- Do not ask someone else to clock in/out for you
- Intentional falsification of location data is grounds for immediate termination
- ORBIT will cooperate with law enforcement if fraud is suspected

### 3.4 Show Up & Communicate
- You agree to show up for shifts you accept
- If you can't make a shift, notify your employer as soon as possible
- If you're delayed or have issues, communicate with your employer
- No-show without communication may result in termination from platform
- Repeated no-shows harm your reputation with employers

### 3.5 Follow Employer Rules
- You must follow your employer's rules and safety guidelines
- You must comply with all laws (labor laws, safety, OSHA, etc.)
- ORBIT is not responsible for injuries or violations caused by your non-compliance
- Report safety issues to your employer immediately

## 4. GPS Verification Terms

**You agree that:**

- ORBIT may capture your GPS location at clock-in and clock-out
- Your GPS location is used to verify you were at the job location
- This ensures accurate payroll and prevents fraud
- Your location data is kept confidential and secure
- Your location is NOT tracked between clock-in and clock-out (not surveillance)
- If you believe GPS data is inaccurate, you can dispute it (see dispute process)

**See "GPS Verification Policy" for complete details.**

### 5.1 How You're Paid
- You are paid for hours worked, as verified by clock-in/out timestamps
- Payment frequency: [Weekly / Bi-weekly] (determined by employer)
- Payment method: Direct deposit to your bank account

### 5.2 Tax Withholding
- ORBIT and your employer are required by law to withhold federal, state, and local taxes
- Your W-2 or 1099 will be provided before tax season

- If you believe your payment is incorrect, contact your employer within 7 days

- Recovery may occur in future paychecks
- You agree to return overpayment if requested

## 6. Data Privacy & Security

### 6.1 What Data Is Collected
- Name, address, phone, email
- Social Security Number (for W-2/tax purposes)
- Bank account info (for direct deposit)
- GPS coordinates at clock-in/out
- Work history and hours
- Payment history
- Any messages or support communications

### 6.2 How Data Is Used
- To process and pay you
- To verify your location during shifts
- To generate W-2/1099 tax forms
- To comply with labor laws
- To improve the ORBIT platform
- To prevent fraud and enforce this agreement

### 6.3 Data NOT Collected
- Your location between clock-in and clock-out (not tracked)
- Your personal browsing history
- Your private communications outside ORBIT
- Your biometric data (Face ID/Touch ID used only for app login)

### 6.4 Data Security
- All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/SSL)
- All data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
- Access controlled by role (only authorized staff)
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Compliance with industry standards (SOC 2, ISO 27001)

### 6.5 Data Sharing
- Your data is NOT shared with third parties
- Exception: Employer receives your name, hours, location (for their shifts)
- Exception: Tax authorities receive required tax data (legal requirement)
- Exception: Law enforcement (with valid court order)
- You can request your data to be deleted (see Data Rights)

### 6.6 Your Data Rights
- Right to Access: Request download of all your data
- Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal data
- Right to Correct: Request corrections to inaccurate data
- Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of non-essential communications
- Contact: privacy@orbitstaffing.io

## 9. Dispute Resolution & Arbitration

### Disputes Will Be Resolved As Follows:

**Step 1: Contact Employer**
- Email or call your employer
- Provide specific details of the dispute
- Give employer 5 business days to respond

**Step 2: Contact ORBIT Support**
- If employer doesn't resolve, contact ORBIT
- Submit detailed complaint with evidence
- ORBIT will investigate within 10 days

**Step 3: Arbitration** (if still unresolved)
- Dispute goes to binding arbitration
- Arbitrator chosen mutually by both parties
- Arbitration is final and enforceable
- Costs split between worker and ORBIT

### You Agree To:
- Resolve disputes through this process (not small claims court)
- Accept arbitration as final decision
- Not file class action lawsuits
- Not pursue collective legal action

**Exception:** You can still file with the Department of Labor or EEOC for wage theft or discrimination.
