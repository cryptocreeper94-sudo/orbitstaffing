## 3. CLIENT OBLIGATIONS

### 3.1 Payment Terms
- **Monthly Subscriptions:** Payment due on or before the 1st of each month
- **Revenue Share Model:** Monthly invoicing based on reported placement revenue
- **Franchise Licenses:** One-time payment upon signature
- **Late Payment:** 1.5% monthly interest on payments overdue by 30+ days
- **Currency:** All payments in USD

### 3.2 Data Accuracy & Management
Client agrees to:
- Provide accurate worker information (name, SSN, address, tax withholding)
- Maintain current client company contact information
- Keep job details and placement terms accurate
- Update worker status when placements end
- Notify ORBIT of any data discrepancies within 5 business days

### 3.3 Compliance & Legal Responsibility
Client is responsible for:
- All hiring and employment compliance (I-9, EVerify, background checks)
- State/federal wage and hour law compliance
- Workers compensation insurance requirements
- Independent contractor vs. employee classification
- Worker safety and OSHA requirements
- Discrimination and harassment prevention

**ORBIT is NOT responsible for:**
- Compliance with Client's specific state regulations (though we provide tools)
- Worker classification decisions
- Wage rate determinations
- Hiring discrimination claims
- Worker safety on client job sites

### 3.4 Proper Platform Usage
Client agrees to:
- Use the platform only for lawful staffing operations
- Not misrepresent worker qualifications or status
- Not attempt to bypass the system or access unauthorized data
- Report any security vulnerabilities immediately
- Maintain login security and not share credentials

### 3.5 Multi-Agency Consolidation (Enterprise Clients)
If consolidating multiple staffing agencies under one ORBIT account:
- Client takes on responsibility for all consolidated agencies
- Client is liable for compliance across all agencies
- Master account holder controls all agency data
- All revenue reporting aggregates to master account

## 4. WORKER OBLIGATIONS

### 4.1 Information Accuracy
Workers agree to:
- Provide accurate personal information (name, SSN, address)
- Report correct tax withholding preferences
- Disclose all relevant work authorizations and licenses
- Keep contact information current

### 4.2 Placement Compliance
Workers agree to:
- Accept assignments in good faith
- Perform work safely and professionally
- Report actual hours worked accurately
- Use mobile app for proper clock in/out
- Not claim hours not actually worked
- Comply with client company rules and safety requirements

### 4.3 GPS & Attendance Verification
Workers agree to:
- Use GPS verification on mobile app for clock in/out
- Understand that clock in requires physical presence at job location (200-300ft radius)
- Accept that falsified attendance is grounds for immediate termination
- Allow accurate time tracking for payroll

### 4.4 Confidentiality
Workers agree to:
- Not share SSN or tax information with other workers
- Keep client company information confidential
- Not solicit jobs/workers outside the ORBIT system
- Not misrepresent qualifications or experience

## 5. DATA SECURITY & PRIVACY

### 5.1 Data Protection
ORBIT commits to:
- Encrypt all SSN and tax data at rest and in transit
- Never share personal data with third parties (except for payroll processing)
- Maintain audit trails of all data access
- Comply with GDPR, CCPA, and state privacy laws
- Regular security updates and penetration testing

### 5.2 Data We Collect
- Worker: Name, SSN, address, phone, tax withholding, work history
- Client: Company name, contacts, locations, contract terms
- Placements: Job details, pay rates, hours, GPS location, attendance

### 5.3 Data Retention
- **Active accounts:** Data retained as long as account is active
- **Closed accounts:** Data retained for 7 years for tax/compliance purposes
- **Upon request:** We can anonymize or delete personal data (except tax records)
- **Compliance:** Tax and payroll data retained per IRS requirements (7+ years)

### 5.4 Breach Notification
If personal data is compromised:
- ORBIT notifies Client within 24 hours
- ORBIT notifies workers within 48 hours
- Full investigation and remediation report within 30 days
- ORBIT covers notification costs

## 13. MULTI-AGENCY & ENTERPRISE ADDENDUM

### 13.1 Hub Consolidation

- Single point of contact for billing and support
- Access to all child agencies' data

- Unified reporting across all agencies

- Real-time dashboard showing all agencies

Parent company can:
- Set billing terms across multiple agencies
- Apply compliance rules uniformly
- Adjust worker rates across agencies (if contractually permitted)

**Limitations:**
- Cannot override individual agency's direct client contracts
- Cannot unilaterally change worker pay (must comply with existing rates)
- Must respect state/federal minimums

### 13.4 Separate Billing (Optional)
If consolidated but wanting separate invoices:
- Each agency receives individual invoice
- Parent account still manages all data access
- Master reporting available at parent level
- Support works through parent account
