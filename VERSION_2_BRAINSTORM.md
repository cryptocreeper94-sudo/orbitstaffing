## MOONSHOT IDEAS (Game Changers)

### 1. **Predictive Worker Dropout (ML)**
- **Idea:** Predict which workers will quit within 2 weeks
- **How:** Analyze attendance patterns, engagement metrics, earnings trajectory
- **Use Case:** Proactive intervention (bonuses, schedule adjustments, mentorship)
- **Impact:** 10%+ improvement in retention
- **Effort:** 4-6 weeks (data science needed)

### 2. **Dynamic Bonus System (AI)**
- **Idea:** AI adjusts bonus amounts based on market, worker, job type
- **How:** ML model learns what bonuses actually drive retention
- **Use Case:** Optimize ROI on bonus spend (only pay what's needed to retain)
- **Impact:** 20-30% more efficient bonus allocation
- **Effort:** 3-4 weeks

### 3. **Job Site Safety AI**
- **Idea:** AI analyzes worker movement patterns on job sites for safety
- **How:** GPS + accelerometer detect risky behavior (falls, near-misses)
- **Use Case:** Alert managers to safety issues in real-time
- **Impact:** Reduce worker injuries by 30-50%
- **Effort:** 6-8 weeks

### 4. **Franchise Replication Engine**
- **Idea:** One-click white-label franchise launch
- **How:** Template all data, branding, workflows for new franchises
- **Use Case:** Launch franchise in 24 hours with pre-configured data
- **Impact:** Reduce franchise setup time from 2 weeks to 1 day
- **Effort:** 4-6 weeks

### 5. **Worker Skill Marketplace**
- **Idea:** Marketplace where workers can auction their time
- **How:** Workers bid on available shifts, best price wins
- **Use Case:** Workers control their price, agencies get competitive rates
- **Impact:** More worker autonomy, better matches
- **Effort:** 8-10 weeks

## SCORING FRAMEWORK (For Prioritization)

**Rate each idea on:**
1. **Impact** (1-10): How much does it improve retention/revenue?
2. **Effort** (1-10): How hard is it to build? (low = easy)
3. **Strategic Fit** (1-10): How aligned with ORBIT's mission?
4. **Market Demand** (1-10): How many customers want this?
5. **Competitive Advantage** (1-10): How unique is this?

**Score = (Impact + Strategic Fit + Market Demand + Competitive Advantage) - (Effort × 2)**

**Top scoring ideas should be prioritized for v2/v3**
