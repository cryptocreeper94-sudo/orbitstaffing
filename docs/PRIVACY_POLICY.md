# ORBIT Staffing OS - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 2025**

## 1. Introduction

ORBIT Staffing OS ("Platform", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our staffing management platform.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide Directly
- **Account Information**: Name, email, phone, business details, PIN
- **Worker Information**: Name, SSN (encrypted), address, background check results, drug test results, certifications
- **Client Information**: Company name, contact details, job requirements
- **Health/Compliance Data**: Drug test results, workers compensation claims, incident reports, medical clearance status
- **Financial Data**: Payment information, invoices, payroll records

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically
- Login activity and timestamps
- GPS location data (for job clock-in/out verification)
- Device information (IP address, browser type)
- Platform usage analytics (feature access, time on platform)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide staffing management services (job matching, assignments, scheduling)
- Process background checks and drug testing
- Generate payroll and invoices
- Comply with legal/regulatory requirements (workers compensation, OSHA, state labor laws)
- Improve platform security and prevent fraud
- Send service notifications and updates
- Generate anonymized analytics (NO personal data shared)

## 4. Data Security

### 4.1 Encryption Standards
- SSN and sensitive personal data: AES-256 encryption at rest
- All data in transit: TLS 1.2+ (HTTPS)
- Drug test results: Encrypted and access-controlled
- Worker compensation files: Encrypted storage

### 4.2 Access Controls
- Role-based access control (Master Admin, Franchise Admin, Customer Admin)
- Multi-tenant isolation (data segregation between franchises/customers)
- PIN-based admin authentication
- Audit logs for all data access

### 4.3 Breach Notification
If a security breach occurs, we will notify affected users within 30 days as required by law.

## 5. Data Retention & Deletion

- **Active User Data**: Retained while account is active
- **Background Checks**: 7 years (compliance requirement)
- **Drug Test Results**: 5 years (DOT/state requirements)
- **Workers Compensation Records**: 7+ years (state law requirement)
- **Payroll Records**: 7 years (tax/employment law)
- **Upon Account Deletion**: Data deleted within 30 days, except where legally required to retain

## 6. Third-Party Data Sharing

We share data ONLY with:
- **Drug Testing Providers** (Concentra, Fast Pace, Busy Bee, others) - For scheduling/results only
- **Background Check Services** - For compliance verification
- **Payment Processors** (Stripe) - For billing only (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **Workers Compensation Carriers** - For claim processing
- **Legal/Law Enforcement** - When required by law

**We DO NOT:**
- Sell personal data to third parties
- Share data with marketing companies
- Share SSN or sensitive health data with anyone except required service providers
- Use data for purposes other than service provision and legal compliance

## 7. State-Specific Requirements

### 7.1 Drug Testing Compliance
- Follows DOT regulations and state drug testing laws
- Chain of custody procedures maintained
- Results handled per SAMHSA guidelines
- Opt-in consent required before testing

### 7.2 Background Check Compliance
- FCRA compliance (Fair Credit Reporting Act)
- Applicant consent required before checks
- Adverse action notice provided if disqualified
- Right to dispute results

### 7.3 Workers Compensation
- Incident reporting HIPAA-compliant
- Medical records encrypted
- Claims data protected per state workers comp laws

### 7.4 Data Localization
- All data stored in US-based servers (Neon PostgreSQL)
- GDPR-compliant for EU users (if applicable)
- CCPA-compliant for California users

## 8. Worker Rights

Workers have the right to:
- Access their own data (upon request)
- Request data deletion (except legally-required retention periods)
- Opt-out of non-essential communications
- File complaints with state labor board

**Data Access Request**: Email support@orbitstaffing.net with "Data Request" in subject

## 9. HIPAA & Health Information

Drug test results and medical information are treated as protected health information (PHI) when applicable. We maintain business associate agreements with covered entities and follow HIPAA Privacy Rule requirements.

## 10. Children's Privacy

ORBIT is intended for users 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via email. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of updated terms.
