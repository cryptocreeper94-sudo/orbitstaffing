## 1. Overview: Two Paths to ORBIT

ORBIT serves two distinct markets with different financial capacity:

### Path A: SUBSCRIPTION (Small/Bootstrapping Agencies)
- Monthly payment model
- For agencies with $100k-500k revenue
- No upfront capital required
- Perfect for founders with hustle, not capital
- Monthly cost based on worker count

### Path B: FRANCHISE (Established Agencies)
- One-time licensing fee
- For agencies with $500k+ revenue or multi-region
- Permanent license (pay once, own forever)
- Lower lifetime cost than subscription
- Regional exclusivity option
