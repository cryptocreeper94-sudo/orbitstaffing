## üì§ UPLOADING TO PLAY STORE

1. Go to Google Play Console
2. Navigate to your app
3. Select "Create new release"
4. Upload APK/AAB file
5. Go to "Store listing" tab
6. Upload icon (512x512px)
7. Upload feature graphic (1024x500px)
8. Upload screenshots (1080x1920px each)
9. Fill in descriptions from GOOGLE_PLAY_STORE_LISTING.md
10. Submit for review
