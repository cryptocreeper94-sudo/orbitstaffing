Recipient may use Confidential Information only for:

❌ **Post** Confidential Information on social media, forums, or public platforms

❌ **Reverse-engineer** technology or systems to discover trade secrets

- Store Confidential Information securely (locked files, encrypted devices)
- Limit access to only authorized personnel with direct need-to-know

## 6. PENALTIES FOR BREACH

### 6.1 Consequences of Violation
If Recipient breaches this NDA:

**Immediate Actions:**
- ORBIT may terminate employment or business relationship immediately
- ORBIT may seek injunctive relief (court order to stop disclosure)
- Recipient liable for all damages resulting from breach

**Financial Penalties:**
- Liquidated damages: $10,000 per breach incident
- All costs of investigation and legal proceedings
- Three times actual damages (treble damages under trade secret law)
- Attorney fees and court costs

**Legal Action:**
- ORBIT can pursue civil lawsuit in Tennessee courts
- ORBIT can report criminal conduct to law enforcement
- Injunctive relief to prevent further disclosure

### 6.2 Specific Violations & Penalties
| Violation | Penalty |
|-----------|---------|
| Unauthorized disclosure to 1 person | $10,000 + damages |
| Disclosure to competitor | $25,000 + damages |
| Disclosure of client list or pricing | $50,000 + damages + client notification |
| Solicitation of ORBIT clients/workers | $15,000 per client/worker + damages |
| Public disclosure (social media, internet) | $50,000+ + damages + reputational harm |
| Trade secret theft | Criminal prosecution + civil damages |

### 6.3 Injunctive Relief
Recipient acknowledges that:
- Monetary damages may be insufficient remedy
- ORBIT entitled to seek injunctive relief (court order)
- Breach may cause irreparable harm
- Recipient will not contest ORBIT's right to seek injunction

## 7. DATA & INFORMATION SECURITY

### 7.1 Data Protection Commitment
ORBIT commits to:
- Encrypt all sensitive data
- Limit access to authorized personnel only
- Maintain secure backups
- Regular security audits
- GDPR and CCPA compliance

### 7.2 Recipient's Security Obligations
Recipient must:
- Use strong passwords (minimum 12 characters)
- Enable two-factor authentication if provided
- Never share login credentials
- Lock computer when away from desk
- Use company VPN for remote access
- Encrypt personal devices storing company data
- Report security incidents within 2 hours

### 7.3 Breach Notification
If Recipient suspects unauthorized access:
1. Notify ORBIT immediately (within 2 hours)
2. Provide details of suspected breach
3. Cooperate fully with investigation
4. Do not attempt to cover up or minimize breach
5. Reimburse ORBIT for notification costs if breach caused by Recipient negligence

## 11. REMEDIES & DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### 11.1 Available Remedies
If Recipient breaches this NDA:

✓ **Injunctive Relief** - Court order to stop disclosure  
✓ **Monetary Damages** - Recovery of actual damages caused  
✓ **Liquidated Damages** - $10,000 per breach (as defined above)  
✓ **Attorney Fees** - ORBIT recovers all legal costs if prevailing  
✓ **Punitive Damages** - Additional damages for willful breach  
✓ **Criminal Prosecution** - For trade secret theft  

### 11.2 Dispute Process
**Step 1: Informal Notice**
- ORBIT sends notice of suspected breach
- Recipient has 5 business days to respond
- Good faith discussion to resolve

**Step 2: Arbitration**
If unresolved:
- Binding arbitration in Tennessee
- Single arbitrator mutually selected
- Costs split 50/50 unless one party wins completely
- Decision binding on both parties
- Limited appeal rights

**Step 3: Court Action**
If necessary:
- Litigation in Tennessee state or federal court
- Loser pays winner's attorney fees
- Injunctive relief available
