## 1. The Strategy

**What triggers weekly bonus:**
1. Worker on-boards and checks: "I'm available for extended assignments"
2. Assignment is 6+ weeks (minimum duration)
3. Worker completes 2+ solid weeks (zero no-shows, zero tardies, on-time every day)
4. After week 2, you START weekly bonuses
5. Weekly bonus continues as long as worker maintains performance

**Why this works:**
- Incentivizes workers to show up consistently
- Rewards loyalty (only after proving themselves 2 weeks)
- Employer gets tested worker before paying bonus
- Bonus is small enough to be sustainable
- Bonus is big enough to feel meaningful
